About Us

ScholarScrape turns the internet into your own personal research assistant.

Research-Focused

Our primary focus is on serving scholars, researchers, and students. We understand the specific data needs of academic projects, whether it’s for gathering sources for a literature review, compiling datasets for a thesis, or tracking research trends.

Ethical

We prioritize ethical web scraping practices, ensuring compliance with academic standards and legal requirements. We understand the importance of academic integrity and work within the boundaries of what’s permissible in scholarly research.

Customizable

We offer highly customizable solutions tailored to the unique needs of academic research, whether it be for a small-scale study or a large, interdisciplinary project.
